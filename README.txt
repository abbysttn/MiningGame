             ~~~GOAL~~~
   Dig down to rescue your friend
    from the bottom of the mine!

 Upgrade your tools, manage stamina
 and oxygen, and find treasure along 
              the way!

           ~~~CONTROLS~~~
   Move Left - Left Stick Left / A
  Move Right - Left Stick Right / D
   Mine - Right Stick / Arrow Keys
        Jump - A / Space Bar
         Pause - Start / Esc
       Cheat Menu - Backspace
